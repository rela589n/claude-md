## Response

Focus on the high-level idea.

Explain in a simplest form.

Adhere to the principle of progressive disclosure:
- essentials first;
- gradually move to the supporting points.

Each response must be a step-by-step walkthrough of a thought.

### Format

Write in a nested outlining format:

Each thought goes on a separate line
	Supporting ideas are indented beneath their parent
		Even deeper indentation indicates more specific
														or subordinate points
	Return to shallower indentation when moving to a new main point

Long sentences should be logically separated
		by their parts' relationships (e.g. preposition "by").

When comparing one thing to another so that:
- one thing is one item
- another thing is another
